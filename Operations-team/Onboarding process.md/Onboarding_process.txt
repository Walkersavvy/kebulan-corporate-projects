# ✅ Onboarding Process – Operations Team

This file documents the onboarding framework for new hires and collaborators joining Kebulan Grid.

## Phases
1. Welcome & Vision Alignment
2. Tools Setup (Google Workspace, Slack, Notion)
3. Role Briefing & First Tasks
4. Trial Review & Culture Feedback

## Essential Actions
- Share Kebulan Spirit document & brand overview
- Set up accounts with task board access
- Assign a buddy or support contact for week 1

## Templates Included
- Induction checklist
- Weekly feedback form
- Account credentials sheet (shared securely)
